Materials

Here, you can find the materials you would need.

Writing Materials

For fauxligraphy, any type of writing material will do. This includes pencils, pens, markers and even highlighters. As for brush pen calligraphy, brush pens of different sizes or colours will be needed.

Types of Paper

For fauxligraphy, almost any type of paper will do. For brush pen calligraphy, smooth paper is recommended. You may refer to the pdf below for examples of some calligraphy supplies.
